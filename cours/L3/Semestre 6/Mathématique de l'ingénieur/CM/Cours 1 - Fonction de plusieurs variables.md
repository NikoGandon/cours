2. $\sqrt{ 4-x^2-y ^2 }$
   
$$\Leftrightarrow \left \{
\begin{array}{rcl}
z = 4 - x^2 - y^2 \\
z \geq 0

z + x^2 + y^2 = 4 \\
z \geq 0
\end{array}
\right.$$
